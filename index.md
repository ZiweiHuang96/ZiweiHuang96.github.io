**I received the Ph.D. degree in Information and Communication Engineering from Peking University, Beijing, China, in 2024. I am currently a Boya Postdoctoral Fellow at Peking University. My general research interests are in areas of wireless communications and artificial intelligence, subject on which I have published more than 30 journal and conference papers, 2 books, and hold 2 patents. I am a recipient of the China National Postdoctoral Program for Innovative Talents. I was a co-recipient of the IET Communications Best Paper Award: Premium Award. I am a group member of [Pervasive Connectivity and Networked Intelligence (PCNI)](http://pcni.pku.edu.cn/homepage.html). My Google Scholar link is [here](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=UO10gm8AAAAJ&hl=zh-CN&oi=sra).**
